Inspired Work

While Junji Ito has been aware of these projects, he is not officially apart of them and they are different from his original work. He thoroughly enjoys everyone who takes inspiration from his work and turns it into new art, and often a Junji Ito easter egg is involved in these inspired works.

Grotesque Beauty

Grotesque Beauty is a horror narrative game featuring branching paths and 30 gruesome endings. The game focuses on building an eerie atmosphere and haunting visuals rather than relying on jump scares. This visual novel is inspired by the work of Junji Ito and Nakayama Masaaki.

World of Horror

World of Horror, also known by its Japanese title Kyōfu no Sekai, is a role-playing video game with a 1-bit pixel art style inspired by the works of H. P. Lovecraft and Junji Ito. The game was created by Polish developer Paweł Koźmiński and published by Ysbryd Games. Developer panstasz added a cameo of Ito-san as "Mr. OOO, the Struggling Artist" and other various Junji Ito references, like Remina.

Girl from Nowhere

Nanno is a mysterious and clever girl who should be avoided when she's in the area. She transfers to different schools, and when she lands at a new institution, she exposes the lies and misdeeds of the students and faculty at every turn. The girls Nanno encounters often want to try to destroy her, but she always has the upper hand. When a student with a girlfriend gets mistakenly linked to Nanno, he becomes a social media sensation. After the truth about Nanno's high school life is revealed, she makes sure there are no happy endings. The lead actress has openly said she's a big junji ito fan and used Tomie as the direct source reference for the part.
