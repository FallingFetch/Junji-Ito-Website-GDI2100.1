Adaptations

A lot of Junji Ito's most well known work has been adapted into movies and TV shows, a lot of which he had a hand in. Some fans have also created adaptations of his work!

Movies

Some of Junji Ito's best-known works such as Tomie, Uzumaki, and Gyo have been made into movies along with many others. Junji Ito is often on the writing team for these projects, and the visuals are outstanding.

Unofficial

Take a look at some of the unofficial fan adaptations of Junji Ito's work. While Junji Ito isn't involved, the love of his work by the fans takes his work to another level with some great changes and acting.

TV Shows

Lots of Junji Ito's best and even lesser-known works have been tuned into episodes of TV shows. These are a great introduction to his work if reading manga isn't for you, or you just want a coloured and voiced adaptation of some of your favourite Junji Ito stories.
