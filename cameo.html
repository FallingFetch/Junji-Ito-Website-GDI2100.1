Cameos

Junji Ito has some cameos in other well-known media. These Easter eggs are always fun to come across, however, you can find some of the cameos he has made below

Junji Ito in Death Stranding

After a lengthy corporate conflict with Konami and cancellation of Silent Hills, Kojima Productions closed in July 2015 and reformed as an independent video game developer and studio in December. The same month, Hideo Kojima announced his partnership with Sony Interactive Entertainment to make a new PlayStation game, Death Stranding was released by Sony Interactive Entertainment for the PlayStation 4 in November 2019. While the game has heavy influence from Silent Hills, starring Norman Reedus and featuring the likeness of Guillermo del Toro, Junji Ito had no involvement in the game. However, Junji Ito's likeness appears as a cameo early in the game as the Engineer.

Junji Ito Crossover with Identity V

The NetEase developed 1v4 asymmetrical survival horror game has had several crossover events with the Junji Ito Collection anime, featuring skins for characters and a map influenced by the anime. This was the first crossover event held in Identity V. In the first stage of this event they released the limited skins: Souichi for Lucky Guy and The Intersection Bishounen for Wu Chang. There was also a limited item for Lucky Guy: Voodoo Doll. The second stage of this event released the limited skin: The Painted Girls for Dream Witch. There was also a limited item for all survivors: Straw Eyeball. It also introduced a new map: Eversleeping Town.The third stage of this event released the limited skin: Tomie Kawakami for Dream Witch. There was also a limited item for all survivors: Straw Eyeball, and a limited pet: Jean Pierre.
